Levels Description in Japanese

Basic Level

Learn fundamental Japanese language skills, including essential vocabulary, basic grammar structures, and essential communication phrases. Build a solid foundation in hiragana and katakana scripts, and develop the ability to engage in simple conversations used everyday situations in Japanese."

Intermediate Level

Deepen your Japanese language proficiency by expanding vocabulary, mastering more complex grammar structures, and enhancing communication skills. Develop the ability to express opinions, and understand more aspects of Japanese culture. Focus on kanji and improve your reading, writing, and conversational abilities.

Advanced Level

Individuals demonstrate mastery of complex grammar, vocabulary, and cultural nuances. Proficient in articulate communication, they engage in sophisticated conversations, write well-structured essays, comprehend nuanced texts, and exhibit a deep understanding of Japan's language and culture.
